Searching For Community

Are you in the Five County area of North Carolina and struggling to find a Gospel of Grace based, Holy-Spirit filled community of believers? We are a few believers meeting regularly on Sundays for worship and fellowship together and would love for you to join us. Maybe you live too far away to get together regularly but would still like the support of knowing you're not alone. Either way, please contact us, we would love to hear from you.
